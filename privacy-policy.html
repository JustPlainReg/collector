Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Mantle ("we", "our", or "us") is a collectibles portfolio tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password.
  • Portfolio data: Items you add to your portfolio, purchase prices, and any price alerts you set.
  • Push notification token: If you enable notifications, we store a device token to send you price alerts.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To display and manage your collectibles portfolio.
  • To send push notifications when a price alert you set is triggered.
  • To improve the app experience.

Third-Party Services

Mantle uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase – Database and authentication. Your data is stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo – App infrastructure and push notification delivery. See Expo's Privacy Policy.
  • eBay Browse API – Used to fetch publicly available market pricing data. No personal information is shared with eBay.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Push Notifications

If you grant permission, we may send push notifications to alert you when a collectible reaches a price target you set. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Mantle is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
