Delete Your Mantle Account

You can delete your Mantle account and all associated data at any time using one of the methods below.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

1. Open Mantle and go to the Profile tab. Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account". You will be asked to confirm before anything is deleted.
3. Confirm deletion. Your account and all portfolio data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request via email

If you are unable to access the app, email us at regislabs424@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • Your portfolio items and purchase history
  • Any price alerts you have set
  • Your push notification token

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
